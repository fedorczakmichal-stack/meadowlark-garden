A calm garden — not medical care

Meadowlark isn't therapy, treatment, or a substitute for talking to someone — and it will never claim to be. If you're struggling and need someone now, please reach out. These lines are free, any time.

Any hourCall 988 or text 988 — the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, free and confidential
Prefer to textCrisis Text Line — text HOME to 741741
Outside the USFind a line near you at findahelpline.com

Crisis help is always free — never behind a paywall or a sign-up. In an emergency, contact your local emergency services.
